How to Play

Ready for some fast-action, mole-whackin' fun? Look no further! Grab a mallet and whack your way to to the top of the leaderboards

  • Use your mouse to click on the moles
  • You will get 20 seconds to whack as many mole's as you can
  • For every successful whack of a mole, you will score 10 points
  • To win, whack your way to the highest score!
